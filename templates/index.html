Unemployment Rate (%)

Percentage of the labor force actively seeking but unable to find work.

CPI Inflation Index

Measures changes in the price level of a basket of consumer goods and services.

GDP (Billions USD)

Total monetary value of all goods and services produced in the U.S. economy.

Federal Funds Rate (%)

The interest rate at which banks lend reserve balances to each other overnight.

10-Year Treasury Yield (%)

Return on U.S. government debt maturing in 10 years, a key benchmark for borrowing costs.

Consumer Sentiment

University of Michigan index measuring consumer confidence in economic conditions.
